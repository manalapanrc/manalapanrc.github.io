Events

From weekly group runs to our signature annual races, the MRC calendar reflects a club that takes both its training and its traditions seriously.

Weekly Schedule

Recurring Runs

Our weekly schedule is the heartbeat of the club. All runs depart from Thompson Park unless otherwise noted.

Every Tuesday — 6:00 AM

Speed Work Tuesday

Structured interval training led by our Head Coach. Workouts include tempo runs, track repeats, and fartlek sessions. All paces accommodated.

Every Thursday — 6:30 AM

Easy Recovery Run

Conversational-pace group run. 4–6 miles on flat terrain. Perfect for active recovery and fellowship. New members especially welcome.

Every Saturday — 6:00 AM

Saturday Long Run

The flagship run of the week. Distances range from 8–20 miles depending on training cycle. Multiple pace groups available.
