Apply for Membership

We accept new members on a rolling basis, with cohorts beginning each spring and fall. Applications are reviewed by the membership committee.

Membership

What We Look For

The Manalapan Run Club is not a casual jogging meetup. We are a committed community of runners who hold ourselves and one another to a high standard of participation, conduct, and mutual support.

That said, we welcome runners of all speeds and experience levels. We don't care about your pace — we care about your commitment. If you show up, put in the work, and treat your fellow members with respect, you belong here.

Requirements
  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • Able to complete a 5K distance (walking permitted)
  • Commit to attending at least 2 group runs per month
  • Agree to abide by the club's Principles and Code of Conduct
  • Annual membership dues of $75 (scholarships available)
  • Attend one prospective member group run before final acceptance
What to Expect
  • Application reviewed within 5 business days
  • Invitation to a prospective member run
  • Brief conversation with a board member
  • Official welcome and onboarding within 2 weeks of acceptance
