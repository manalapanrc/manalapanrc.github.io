About the Club

Since 2025, The Manalapan Run Club has upheld a tradition of athletic discipline, community stewardship, and the unwavering belief that running makes us better.

Our Story

From a Small Group of Runners to a Respected Institution

The Manalapan Run Club began as a way for the Manalapan High School cross country team to bond and encourage each other to become runners. Since that fateful day in 2025, the Club intends to expand and spread its message to the Garden State.

Timeline

Milestones

2025
Founded

Six founding members of Manalapan High School's cross country team establish the club with Saturday morning runs at the Manasquan Reservoir.

2026
Growth

Social media pages are established and membership skyrockets to 30+ members.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These values aren't aspirational. They are operational. Every decision we make as a club is filtered through these commitments.

⚡

Discipline

We always show up no matter the conditions and stay in zone 2.

🤝

Fellowship

We run alone sometimes, but we train together always. The bonds formed over shared suffering are the strongest bonds there are.

🎯

Excellence

We compete with yesterday's version of ourselves. Personal records matter not for vanity, but as evidence of intentional growth.

🏘️

Stewardship

We serve the community that hosts us. Every mile we run through Manalapan is a mile of gratitude for the place we call home.

🔑

Integrity

We are honest in our training, respectful in our competition, and accountable in our conduct. Character is non-negotiable.

🌱

Growth

Every member, regardless of pace or experience, is on a journey. We honor the process and celebrate progress at every level.
